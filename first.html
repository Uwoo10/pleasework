Riverman

Leaves shake to shine. The shadows of them do sometimes appear to be shape of a man. I'm going to call it "Riverman." He always sounds like rainfall. At first, Riverman was of the scenary of my window. I didn't knew why he was there, but now I think he was screaming out, like "Look at me!" It was faint like a moan of a man with sleep paralysis. From fall to winter, I cannot find him anywhere. Instead, the sunlight steps to the deepest part of my room. Someone must think the autumn leaves are the pieces of Riverman, but it's not true, I think. They don't shine as he did. Then, where did he go?

During the rainy season, Riverman become bigger and bigger to cover the whole city, but nobody can see him. Awaken and wandering, he gets mad and screams out.
